Game

Jumpy: Multiplication Word Problems

Play
Learners help Roly get across the river by solving single-digit multiplication word problems in this third-grade math game! In Jumpy: Multiplication Word Problems, players will need to apply their knowledge of multiplication to real-world situations. Once they have the correct answer to the word problem, learners will help Roly jump across the river to get to the party! (Hint: Use the space bar to move Roly through the game.)
